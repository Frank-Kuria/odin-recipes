Kimitu Recipe

Description

Kimitu is mashed Potatoes with peas.

Ingredients

  • Mutton
  • Spinach
  • Potatoes
  • peas
  • Onions, Tomatoes, Garlic, Green Pepper, Oil and salt

Steps

  1. Prepare your Ingredients, peel potatoes and chop the vegetables.
  2. Boil the peas in salty water to maintain the colour
  3. In a bigger sufura, boil your potatoes until well done. Add in the peas and simmer for 5 mins. Drain any excess water and mash them up. Add salt to taste.
  4. In a pan, saute onions and add garlic, tomatoes and pepper. Stir until cooked. Add in the mutton, salt and mix well to incorporate them. Add half a cup of water and cook on medium heat until well done.
  5. Use a tbsp of cornfour to thicken the sauce
  6. Put the spinach in a sufuria, drizzle oil on top and sprinkle onion rings and salt. On medium heat, stir them for 2-4 mins and turn off the fire
  7. Serve and enjoy your meal

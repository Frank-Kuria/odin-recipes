Mukimo Recipe

Description

Mukimo is a meal whose origin is central Kenya but its being served in Hotels across the country.

Ingredients

  • 1 kg Irish Potatoes
  • 2 handfuls of pumpkin leaves
  • 3 cups of soft maize off the cob
  • A bunch of spring onions
  • A spoonful of cooking oil
  • salt

Steps

  1. Carefully peel the potatoes and slice them into halves
  2. Prepare the pumpkin leaves by removing the stalk and shred them into sizeable pieces
  3. Mix the pumpkin leaves, maize and potatoes in a sufuria and add water
  4. Place the sufuria on fire and let the mixture boil for 30 minutes under medium heat. Open and add a pinch of salt and let it cook for 10 more minutes
  5. Reduce the heat and mash everything together using a wooden spoon until all blends in well. Remove from Heat and set it aside
  6. In another larger pot, pour in the spring onions and cooking oil and place on fire. Mix until the onions turn golden brown the adjust the heat to minimal and add the Mashed mixture. Mix well
  7. You can now remove from the heat and serve while hot
